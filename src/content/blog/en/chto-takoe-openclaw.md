> Six months ago I was chatting with ChatGPT. At first it seemed like the future. Then it started lying to me using my own facts.

You tell it about your business, discuss numbers, make a decision - and a week later it gives you fiction based on your own data. Its memory doesn't work the way I needed it to.

Then, in early February, I saw an Instagram reel about a **revolution** happening. Not somewhere in the future - right now. I started digging - and found OpenClaw.

---

#### 🔷 What it actually is

OpenClaw is an open-source platform that turns AI models into real AI agents.

Not "asked - answered". But agents that:

- **Live** in your Telegram, WhatsApp or Discord
- **Remember** everything - context, decisions, people, tasks
- **Work** 24/7, even while you sleep
- **Independently** check analytics, inventory, publish content
- **Team** - they work with each other

And most importantly - you can connect **PostgreSQL with semantic search**. The agent doesn't just "remember recent messages" - it searches by meaning across all history. No more hallucinations about your own facts.

> Everything runs on your computer. Your data stays with you 🔒

#### 🔷 How I got here

I'm an entrepreneur from Kemerovo, Russia. Electronics, vape shops, equipment rental. I have tons of tasks that can be automated - not by hiring people, but with AI.

On YouTube I found [Alexey Ulyanov](https://youtube.com/@alekseiulianov) - he shows in detail and in practice how everything works. And [@denpleada](https://t.me/denpleada) showed how AI copilots actually work in business.

I thought: what if I build a whole team of agents for my tasks? 🤔

---

#### 🔷 Honest about the journey

> Bought a Claude Max subscription for $100. No turning back.

**My advice:** buy the subscription first, then set things up. When money's on the line - motivation is completely different 💪

First day I set everything up on my MacBook via terminal. With Gemini's help, step by step. The whole day. And when it finally worked - I experienced real *catharsis* 🎉

But then - debugging. Constant. Proxies go down, subscriptions get blocked. One of the GPT agents got into a loop - sent me 50 identical messages in a row 😅

> Don't spend big money on courses. Better to spend time understanding how things work. Then you actually know how to use it.
